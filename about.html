Our Story

A journey of flavors, passion, and community since 2010. Discover the legacy behind every bite.

The Feane Legacy

It all started in a small kitchen with a big dream. Our founder, Marcus Feane, believed that fast food shouldn't mean a compromise on quality. He set out to prove that gourmet techniques could be applied to casual dining, creating a menu that was both accessible and extraordinary.

Today, Feane has grown into a city-wide favorite, known for our commitment to "Fresh First" sourcing and our "Flavor Forever" philosophy. We've stayed true to our roots, using the same secret family recipes that first made us a neighborhood gem.

Our Core Values

Fresh Ingredients

We partner with local farmers to ensure every ingredient is farm-to-table fresh, every single day.

Made with Love

Cooking is our passion. Every burger is flipped, and every pizza is tossed with genuine care.

Community First

Supporting our neighbors through local events and sustainable practices is at the heart of what we do.

Our Mission

To redefine the fast-food experience by delivering gourmet-quality meals that are fast, fresh, and full of flavor, fostering moments of joy for every guest who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To become the global gold standard for elevated casual dining, where tradition meets innovation, and Every meal tells a story of craftsmanship and quality.
